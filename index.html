About Us

The Learning and Interactive Robotics (LIVE) Lab at the University of Virginia focuses on developing intelligent robotic systems that can safely and effectively operate in unstructured environments. We explore the intersection of machine learning, control theory, and human-robot interaction to build robots that learn from and adapt to humans.
